# Strategic Yield Optimization Analysis
**Digital Inventory & Yield Analytics**  
**Date:** February 2025  
**Analyst:** Sri Harshavardhan Josyula

## Executive Summary
Analysis of 550,000+ ad impression records across Q4 2024 identifies **$6.3M in revenue 
optimization opportunities** through targeted yield management strategies. Sports and 
Entertainment verticals represent 48% of total revenue loss.

## Key Findings

| Metric | Value | Status |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Total Revenue Loss | $6.34M | Critical |
| Under-Pacing Rate | 22.3% | At Risk |
| Recoverable Revenue | $4.8M | Opportunity |
| Current Avg Yield | 76.8% | Below Target |

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Pacing Correction Protocol (Impact: $1.7M recovery)
- **Target:** 122,000 units with &lt;85% pacing
- **Action:** Automated alerts by Day 7 of campaign
- **Owner:** Inventory Operations

### 2. Dynamic Packaging Strategy (Impact: $1.2M recovery)
- **Bundle:** Under-performing Entertainment + high-demand Sports
- **Target:** CTV and Streaming devices (highest CPM)
- **Timeline:** Month 2 implementation

### 3. Audience Optimization (Impact: $900K recovery)
- **Focus:** Expand "Parents" segment (92% yield rate)
- **Reduce:** "Sports Fans" over-delivery (68% yield rate)
- **Method:** Lookalike modeling and auto-shifting

- **Dashboard:** Executive visibility into KPIs
